What is Magenta Hardcore? It's whatever the process makes it mean. Thus far it has manifested as a continual experiment in pushing art to its death and unlife, communizing, trending towards 0, and explicit demon worship. Although Magenta Hardcore is ostensibly under my control and influence, it has proven itself to be a mode of transport merely summoned through me as a means. Magenta Hardcore is concerned with acceleration and all that comes with it. It is a time sorcery bound to nothingness.
